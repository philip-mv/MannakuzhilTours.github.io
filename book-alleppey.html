Alleppey Houseboat Tour Packages – Full Itinerary Details (2026)

Experience the best of Alleppey backwaters with our private houseboat packages. All cruises are on traditional kettuvallam boats with private crew, authentic Kerala cuisine, and scenic routes through Vembanad Lake, Punnamada Lake, and narrow canals. Prices are fixed per boat (not per person) – perfect for families, couples, and groups.

1. Alleppey Day Cruise – 6-7 Hour Backwater Tour

Best for: Day trippers, short visits to Kerala backwaters

Duration: 6–7 hours | Price: Approx ₹12,000 total (private boat)

  • Check-in: 11:00 AM at Pallathuruthy / Finishing Point Jetty, Alleppey
  • Cruise Route: Vembanad Lake → Punnamada Lake → Punchiri Jetty → R/C Blocks paddy fields
  • Highlights: Palm-fringed canals, village life, coir-making units, toddy shops, traditional Kerala fishing
  • Lunch: Fresh onboard Kerala meal (fish/chicken/veg options)
  • Relaxation: Scenic views, bird watching, photography
  • Check-out: 5:00–5:30 PM

2. 1 Night Deluxe Houseboat Package – Overnight Kerala Backwater Cruise

Best for: Couples, honeymooners, short romantic getaway

Duration: ~21–22 hours | Price: Approx ₹15,000 total (private boat)

  • Check-in: 12:00 Noon at Alleppey jetty
  • Day Cruise: Punnamada Lake (Nehru Trophy snake boat race venue) → Vembanad Lake → Kainakary village narrow canals
  • Meals: Lunch + evening tea/snacks + authentic Kerala dinner onboard
  • Evening: Anchor at scenic riverside spot ~5:30 PM for sunset views
  • Overnight: Stay on deluxe houseboat with comfortable beds & private bathroom
  • Next Morning: Sunrise cruise + breakfast → Check-out ~9:00–9:30 AM

3. 2 Nights Premium Houseboat Package – Complete Alleppey Backwater Experience

Best for: Families, groups, in-depth Kerala backwater exploration

Duration: ~45–48 hours | Price: Approx ₹32,000 total (private boat)

  • Day 1: Check-in 12:00 Noon → Cruise Vembanad Lake & Pamba River → Overnight in peaceful spot
  • Day 2: Full day in Kuttanad region (Rice Bowl of Kerala) – deeper canals, village walks, optional canoe ride, local life
  • Meals: All 3 meals/day + evening snacks – fresh Kerala cuisine
  • Highlights: Multiple sunsets/sunrises, bird watching, traditional Kerala culture
  • Day 3: Morning cruise + breakfast → Check-out ~9:00 AM
