Trip Details & Typical Itinerary

All packages are **private houseboat** (exclusive for your group). Prices are fixed per boat (common for Alleppey), not per person. Includes onboard Kerala meals, staff, and cruise. Routes may vary slightly based on water levels/weather for safety.

Day Cruise (6-7 hours) – Approx ₹12,000 total
  • Check-in: 11:00 AM (e.g., Pallathuruthy / Finishing Point Jetty)
  • Cruise through palm-fringed canals, Vembanad Lake, Punchiri Jetty, R/C Blocks (man-made paddy fields)
  • Lunch break (~1 hour) at scenic spot (e.g., Marthandom Kayal) – Fresh Kerala cuisine (fish/chicken/veg curry)
  • Relax, watch village life, coir making, paddy fields, coconut groves
  • Disembark: ~5:00–5:30 PM
1 Night Deluxe (~21-22 hours) – Approx ₹15,000 total
  • Check-in: 12:00 Noon
  • Cruise through Punnamada Lake (Nehru Trophy venue), Vembanad Lake, narrow canals, Kainakary village
  • Lunch onboard, afternoon relaxation, tea/snacks
  • Anchor ~5:30 PM in scenic riverside spot for sunset
  • Dinner onboard (authentic Kerala thali)
  • Overnight stay on boat
  • Early morning cruise (~7:30–9:00 AM), breakfast, check-out ~9:00–9:30 AM
2 Nights Premium (~45-48 hours) – Approx ₹32,000 total
  • Day 1: Check-in 12:00 Noon → Cruise Vembanad Lake, Pamba River, SNDP Canal → Overnight in scenic spot
  • Day 2: Full day deeper backwaters (Kuttanad region, lesser-known canals, village life, optional canoe ride) → Sunset views → Overnight
  • Day 3: Sunrise/morning cruise → Breakfast onboard → Check-out ~9:00 AM
  • Includes all meals, more relaxation, village walks (optional)
