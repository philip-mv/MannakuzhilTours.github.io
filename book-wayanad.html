Wayanad Tour Packages – Mannakuzhil Tours

Discover the Wild Beauty of Wayanad – Kerala’s Green Mountain Escape

Wayanad is a lush hill district in the Western Ghats, known for dense forests, rolling hills, waterfalls and a pleasantly cool climate. Our Wayanad tour packages are crafted for travellers who love nature, wildlife and scenic drives.

From peaceful stays in forest resorts to treks, viewpoints and off‑beat routes, Wayanad offers a refreshing break from city life for couples, families and groups alike.

Why Choose Our Wayanad Tour Packages?

  • Nature‑rich sightseeing: Visit viewpoints, dams, waterfalls, caves and forest areas for a complete Wayanad experience.
  • Handpicked nature stays: Stay at hillside resorts, cottages and homestays surrounded by greenery, birdsong and mist.
  • Adventure & outdoor fun: Short treks, plantation walks, jeep rides and optional wildlife safaris based on your comfort level.
  • Great for families & groups: Flexible itineraries that balance relaxation with adventure activities.
  • Easy transfers & local support: Pickup, drop and sightseeing with experienced drivers and local guidance.

Highlights of Our Wayanad Packages

  • Visits to viewpoints, waterfalls and forest routes
  • Tea/coffee plantation walks and village‑side drives
  • Pickup options from Calicut, Mysore or nearby cities
