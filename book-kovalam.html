Kovalam Package

About Kovalam

Kovalam is one of Kerala’s most famous beach towns, known for its crescent‑shaped beaches, lighthouse views and relaxed seaside cafes.

This package can include visits to Lighthouse Beach, Hawa Beach and Samudra Beach, with optional Ayurvedic massages and water activities.

  • Ideal trip duration: 1–3 nights
  • Best time to visit: October – March
  • Can be combined with Trivandrum city sightseeing
